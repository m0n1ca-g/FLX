Our Story

FLX offers versatile pieces designed for premium comfort and style for life at home or on-the-go. FLX offers stylish, active separates in a broad range of sizes because we know all bodies are created differently sized and equally beautiful.

Stylish, High-Quality Athleisure Separates

Designed for premium comfort and style, each separate, from our comfy traveler pant to our multi-use loungewear to our packable jacket, will feel like it was made just for you.

A Destination For Your Active & Casual Lifestyle
